Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 September 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Missing Persons SA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application that provides information about missing persons in South Africa. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding it.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information from the SAPS Official Website:

Our app displays publicly available data about missing persons sourced from the South African Police Service (SAPS) official website. This data includes names, last known locations, descriptions, and other details provided by SAPS.

b. User-Provided Information:

Account Information: When you create an account in our app, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other details you choose to provide.

Comments: You may post comments or provide information related to missing persons. These comments are publicly visible and may be viewed by other users.

3. How We Use Your Information

Profile Information: We use your profile information to create and manage your account, and to personalize your experience within the app.

Comments: Comments are publicly displayed within the app and are intended to assist in the search for missing persons. Your comments may help other users or authorities.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Public Data: Information about missing persons from the SAPS website is publicly available and will be shown within the app.

User Comments: Any comments you post will be publicly visible within the app.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to legal requests from public authorities.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no internet-based service can be completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage your account settings and profile information within the app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ronaldnt8@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

11. External Links

Our app may contain links to external websites that are not operated by us. We have no control over the content and practices of these websites and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.
